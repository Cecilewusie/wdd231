Site Name

Blacq Shot - A photography and videography portfolio showcasing creative visual storytelling.

Site Purpose

Blacq Shot is designed to display professional photography and videography works, provide service details, and enable potential clients to get in touch for bookings.

Scenarios

  • How can I view the photography and videography portfolio?
  • What services does Blacq Shot offer for events like weddings and graduations?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Black (#000) - Used for the background.
  • Secondary Color: White (#fff) - Used for text and highlights.
  • Accent Color: Tomato Red (#ff6347) - Used for hover effects and call-to-action buttons.

Typography

Font: Arial, sans-serif - Used for all text to ensure readability.

Pages & Features

  • Home: Introduction, hero banner, and featured works.
  • Gallery: Showcase of photography and videography projects.
  • Service: Details of photography and videography services.
  • Contact: Contact form for inquiries and bookings.
